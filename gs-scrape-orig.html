Sponsor Sanjay

Meet the man who could change the way hearts heal forever.

Professor Sanjay Sinha is one of our leading British Heart Foundation funded researchers. He is working tirelessly with his team to create a Heart Healing Patch.

This patch has the power to save millions of people living with heart failure and mend broken hearts forever.

Made of stem cells, the Heart Healing Patch could be applied to the heart like a plaster to help it repair.

It's a mind-blowing breakthrough that would offer hope to 64 million people worldwide living with heart failure.

But that's not all Sanjay's doing.

To help raise money and awareness for research into regenerative medicine, including the Heart Healing Patch, Sanjay will be running the TCS London Marathon 2022.

This is the first time hes taken on a marathon, so these 26 miles will not be easy. And he needs your support to get the Heart Healing Patch over the finish line.

This is our chance to make history

As the chosen Charity of the Year for the TCS London Marathon 2022, we have a once-in-a-generation opportunity to champion breakthrough research and offer hope to millions of people living with heart failure across the UK.

And we want you to be part of it

So, while Sanjay is busy getting ready for the marathon, were calling on you to help us celebrate this rare moment in our history by sponsoring Sanjay or taking on The 26 Challenge and raising vital funds for research into regenerative medicine.
